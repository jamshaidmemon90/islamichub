About Islamic Hub

Welcome to Islamic Hub, a modern, fast, and accessible digital platform designed to provide authentic Islamic resources to Muslims worldwide.

This project was built with a core philosophy: Simplicity and Independence. Our entire platform is built using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It does not rely on heavy frameworks or backend servers, meaning it can be run completely offline simply by opening the files in a browser.

Our Mission

To make the Holy Quran, authentic Hadith collections, and daily Azkar easily accessible, beautiful to read, and quick to navigate on any device.

Features

  • Complete Holy Quran with multiple translations.
  • Authentic Hadith collections (Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, etc.).
  • Smart offline Islamic Assistant for quick references.
  • Real-time Prayer Times and Qibla direction.
  • Dark Mode and customizable reading settings.
